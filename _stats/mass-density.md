**Mass and density functions**

Mass function

The probability mass function (p.m.f.) is the histogram of the
distribution, that is:

\- x-axis: values

\- y-axis: frequency

Density function

The probability density function (p.d.f.) is the \"smooth histogram\" of
the distribution.
